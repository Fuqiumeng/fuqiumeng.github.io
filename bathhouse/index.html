The bubbles froth up, a fragrant scent fills the air.

A place to unwind, to indulge and reminisce.

In this little oasis, where my mind can wander.

The world outside fades, as I slip into a dream.

A space to relax and ease the weary mind.
Nothing in the world is as soft and yielding as water.

Yet for dissolving the hard and inflexible,

nothing can surpass it.
The soft overcomes the hard; the gentle overcomes the rigid.
